# Water Access Monitoring Dashboard (Power BI) — Maji Ndogo

A Power BI monitoring dashboard designed to support stakeholders and decision-makers tracking **water source improvement** work in **Maji Ndogo**. The report focuses on **project progress**, **spending transparency**, **budget monitoring**, and **drill-down analysis** across geographic levels (national → provincial → town).

---

## What This Dashboard Answers

This project was built to provide clear visibility into:

- **Current project progress status**
- **Total expenditure to date**
- **Spending breakdown by location**
- **What funds have been used for**
- **Budget forecasting** (whether allocated budget will cover project completion)
- **Potential cost reduction areas**
- **National / provincial / town-level views**
- High-level insight into what field teams are doing on the ground

## Core Features (What Reviewers Should Look At)

### Monitoring & Finance KPIs
- Progress tracking visuals
- Total spend vs. budget tracking (including running/cumulative measures)
- Spending breakdowns by geography and category (as designed in the report)

### Drill-Down Reporting
- National overview, then drill into provincial and town-level insights
- Filters/slicers to quickly isolate areas of interest

### Transparency & Accountability
- Clear breakdown of **where funds went** and **what impact is being achieved**
